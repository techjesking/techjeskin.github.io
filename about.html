SPAN

supported the implementation of NHVMAS conference. It provided the organisation with lots of IEC materials, free samples of condom and lubricants, on HIV and new prevention technology as well as the documentary (In women's hands) to help facilitate the session on HIV prevention. The conference engaged representatives from 14 schools where human physiology were been taught as a discipline. It was estimated that about a 1,000 students and members of the University of Ibadan environ were reached through seminars, and awareness creation walk
